Rich Dad's Cashflow Quadrant: Rich Dad's Guide to Financial Freedom

by Robert T. Kiyosaki and Sharon L. Lechter (2009, Paperback, Spanish Edition)

Do you have financial freedom?

The Rich Dad's Cashflow Quadrant was written for you if your life has reached a financial crossroads. If you want to take control of what you do to change your financial destiny, it will be useful to plan your route.

This book will reveal why some people work less, earn more, pay less taxes and have greater financial security than others. The answer is simple: the secret is to know in which section of the cash flow quadrant we will work and at what specific time we will do it.

The Rich Dad's Cashflow Quadrant has been written for all those who do not wish to remain tied to job security; for people who are ready to undertake transcendental changes in the world in which we live, where information is essential to conquer financial freedom.

The Rich Dad's Cashflow Quadrant deals with the four different types of people that make up the business world, who they are, and what makes the individuals in each quadrant unique. It will help you determine where in the quadrant you are currently and plan the route to where you want to be in the future, by choosing your own path to financial freedom.
